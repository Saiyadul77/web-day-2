My Reading Rom

Author: Md. Saiyadul Amin Akhand

Questions

  1. What is called a reading room?
  2. Where does the reading room stand?
  3. What necessary things are there in a reading room?
  4. How is your reading room?
  5. How do you feel in your reading room?

The term "Reading Room" means a room in which a student or a person goes on with his studies. It is a room which is generally used for reading and writing. I am a student. So I have a reading room of my own. My reading room is not so big and not so small but every beautiful to took at. It is on the south-west side of our main building. It is on the ground room. So it is an airy and well lighted room. I have a chair , a table and a book shelf in my reading room. In the book shelf I keep my books in rows. There is a table clock on my table that helps me to do my duty in proper time. In one corner of my reading room I have kept a flower stand. The sweet smell of flowers charm me all along. I always try my best to keep my reading room neat and clean. I FEEL COMFORTABLE IN IT. I like my reading room very much.

My Hobby

Author: Md. Saiyadul Amin Akhand

Questions

  1. What is called hobby?
  2. What are the advantages of getting a hobby?
  3. How can one choose a hobby?
  4. What is your hobby?
  5. Why do you choose the hobby?

Hobby means doing something wilfully. To spend leisure time every manchooses a hobby according to his taste. I am a student. My hobby is gardening. So, I have made a garden. I work in my garden in the morning and in the evening. I enjoy much interest in working in my garden. I make the soil loose and put out the weeds and grasses with a sickle. At the time of need I water the plants. To protect the tender plants from cows, goats, chickens and naughty children. I have made a strong fence around the garden. It is rich in flowers. My hobby is very interresting and helpful to me. It has given me a cance to make hard physical and mental labour to build my health properly. The sweet scent and beauty of the flower charm me. My hobby is a source of a great pleasure.
